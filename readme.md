## Control to number (4 bits):
- 1: 0
- 2: 1
- A: 2
- B: 3
- Up: 4
- Down: 5
- Left: 6
- Right: 7
- Plus: 8
- Minus: 9
- Home: 10
- Shake: 11
- Tilt-X Back: 12
- Tilt-X Forward: 13
- Tilt-Y Back: 14
- Tile-Y Forward: 15

## Key Up / Down:
- Up: 0,
- Down: 1

## Organization:

__Client -> Server:__

(control) (start/stop)

(----)(-)

__Server -> Client:__

(control) (start/stop) (player #)
